About Us

We are a global community of believers, committed to spreading the message of Jesus Christ and empowering people to live a life of purpose.

MISSION

To fulfill the Great Commandment and Great Commission of the Lord Jesus Christ according to the Great Plan (Mark 12: 30-31; Mathew 28:19-20; Acts 1:8)

VISION

Prophetic Embassy Anointed Church of All Nations envisions a world transformed by the power of God’s love and grace. We strive to foster spiritual growth, empower communities, and spread the Gospel globally, uniting believers from all walks of life in worship, service, and love

STRATEGIES

The Prophetic Embassy Anointed Church of All Nations shall accomplish its mission through the following key strategies: 1. Evangelizing the Unsaved. 2. Establishing Churches. 3. Edifying Believers. 5. Equipping Leaders. 6. Exercising Social Compassion.

CORE VALUES

As a community of believers, we are guided by the following core values: 1.Biblical Authority, We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God 2.Faith, We trust in God's sovereignty, goodness, and faithfulness. 3.Integrity, We seek to live lives of honesty, transparency, and accountability. 4.Servanthood, We seek to serve our community and world in practical ways. 5.Holiness, We seek to live lives that are pleasing to God. These core values guide our decisions, shape our culture, and inspire our mission.
