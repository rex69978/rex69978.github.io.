About Us

Urban Music Promo is dedicated to promoting Malawi's thriving urban music scene by supporting local talents and artists. Our mission is to create a wider audience base for Malawian urban music, helping artists gain more exposure both locally and internationally. We aim to uplift urban music culture and inspire creativity and connection across the country.

Whether it's hip-hop, R&B, Afrobeat, or fusion, Urban Music Promo is committed to giving Malawian artists the spotlight they deserve.
